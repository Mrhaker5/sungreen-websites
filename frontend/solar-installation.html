Why is Professional Installation Crucial?

Poorly installed panels can lead to roof leaks, low energy yield, and even fire hazards. Our solar panel installation in Ranchi uses only UV-protected cables, aluminum/hot-dip galvanized mounting structures, and industrial-grade earthing to ensure your rooftop solar solutions last for 25+ years.

Ranchi experiences heavy monsoons and high temperatures. We use specific tilt angles (usually 15-20 degrees south-facing) to ensure that the dust is washed away by rain and sunlight capture is at its peak during the winter months.
